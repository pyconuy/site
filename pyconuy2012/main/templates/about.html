'About' What is PyCon Uruguay?'

PyCon is the annual conference for the community using and developing the open-source Python programming language. PyConUy 2012 is the first edition of the PyCon event organized by the Uruguayan Python community that mainly seeks to introduce and promote this wonderful language in Uruguay and the region. Our goal is to offer universal access to Python and related tools, that's why we keep the registration at FREE cost, thanks to many volunteers and sponsors.
